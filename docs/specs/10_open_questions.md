1. What is the initial external funding source?
2. What are the exact probe thresholds for finalized lag and chain lag?
3. What evidence standard is sufficient for Same Operator Group classification?
4. Is Program Agent mandatory only for v0.1 MVP, or permanently required beyond v0.1?

## Secondary
5. Should ASN diversity be used as a hard cap or a scoring factor only?
6. What transparency level should raw scoring data have?
7. What reserve policy should be applied to undistributed rewards?
8. How much raw endpoint and probe data can be disclosed without creating operator risk?

## Out of Scope for Initial Version
- fee-based SSNP funding without separate governance approval
- delegated influence
- protocol rule changes
- score inputs based primarily on CPU or ICMP ping
